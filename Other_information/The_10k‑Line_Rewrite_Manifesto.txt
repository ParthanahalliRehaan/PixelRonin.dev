When preparing to build a web app, developers should ask a structured set of questions that cover 
**vision, scope, architecture, and execution**. These questions help avoid costly rework and ensure alignment with stakeholders. 
Here’s a comprehensive checklist:

---

## 🧭 Product & Vision
- What problem does this app solve?
- Who is the target audience?
- What is the **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)** scope?
- What are the success metrics (KPIs)?
- Are there competitors or benchmarks to study?

---

## 🏗️ Architecture & Design
- What tech stack will be used (frontend, backend, database)?
- Should it be monolithic or microservices?
- How will the **directory structure** be organized for scalability?
- What design patterns (MVC, MVVM, etc.) fit best?
- Will it be responsive/mobile-first?

---

## 🔒 Security & Compliance
- How will authentication/authorization be handled?
- What data privacy laws apply (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)?
- How will sensitive data be stored and encrypted?
- What logging and monitoring practices will be in place?

---

## ⚙️ Development Workflow
- What version control strategy (Git flow, trunk-based)?
- How will CI/CD pipelines be set up?
- What coding standards and linting rules will be enforced?
- How will testing be structured (unit, integration, E2E)?
- What environments are needed (dev, staging, prod)?

---

## 📦 Infrastructure & Deployment
- Where will the app be hosted (cloud provider, on-prem)?
- How will scalability be handled (load balancing, caching)?
- What database strategy (SQL vs NoSQL, indexing)?
- How will backups and disaster recovery be managed?

---

## 🎨 UX & UI
- What design system or framework (Material UI, Tailwind)?
- How will accessibility (WCAG compliance) be ensured?
- What user flows are critical for MVP?
- How will feedback loops be integrated?

---

## 📊 Analytics & Growth
- What analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)?
- How will user behavior be tracked?
- What is the plan for A/B testing?
- How will onboarding and retention be measured?

---

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Team & Collaboration
- Who owns product decisions?
- What project management tool (Jira, Trello)?
- How will communication be structured (Slack, standups)?
- What is the release cycle (weekly, bi-weekly)?
