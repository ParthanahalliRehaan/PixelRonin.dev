## Vision

**Problem:** Developers don't know what to learn next or in what order.
**Solution:** PixelRonin is an AI guide that gives you a personalised roadmap and explains each step as you go.
**Audience:** You + a few devs publicly at launch.
**MVP:** One thing only —

> **A user fills the intake form → AI generates their personalised 30-day roadmap → they can track progress.**

Chat, streaming, recommendations — all cut from MVP. Ship that one loop first.
